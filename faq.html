01How do I know JourneyBid is cheapest?

Our model simply opens up your travel needs to more vendors than ever before, adding more competition. The bidding system allows you to select your best rate whilst also getting the travel vendor a booking they may not have otherwise got. It’s truly win-win as you get a competitive costing whilst the vendor will fill more bookings.

02What is your commission? Is there a joining fee? Any hidden charges?

There is no charge to our customers. Our only charge is a flat rate of 10% of the value of each journey, paid by the transport provider. We’re really proud to provide all our service subscription free.

03How do I know JourneyBid gives the best choice?

Traditional phone book call-rounds and online searches are time intensive, especially if you seek the best value comparisons. We have taken all that hassle out with our unique platform to bring the business deals to you. Also, with all the best suppliers from larond the company on JourneyBid.

04What happens if the driver does not show up?

As with all normal service level agreements between hirer and the transport provider, it’s in everyone’s interest the provider will show up. That said however, we employ a very strict code of behaviour and service for all our customers, vetted each and every vendor as they are recruited. Thus any poor service experiences delivered via our partners will be treated with great seriousness and poor performing companies removed.

05What security is there?

All companies are insured and legal to carry passengers as per the law and regulations of the time. We monitor our business performance too and work hard to ensure you get not only the best rates and choice, though also the highest service levels as well.

06Which cities or counties does JourneyBid work in?

JourneyBid is committed to quality delivery thus we are carefully rolling out our services from the main Irish cities. However the beauty of our service is actually in allowing more rural based businesses and customers to make use of our platform promote their service cost free or to find the best deals in your area and to your needs.

07What other services does JourneyBid offer?

Currently we are focused on transport of groups, as the key benefit to business and groups. Though plans are underway to broaden the scope further, though not immediately. The focus now is on high value transport options for groups.

08Is it like Uber or Hailo, just for buses?

Yes and no. We’re similar in that it’s a platform, though not the same in that other companies have a set rate and we are inviting providers to bid their best rate. This enforces value and competitivness for all concerned. That’s what makes JourneyBid perhaps more unique in that providers bid for their roles.

09Will this kill off the bus and coach business?

Not at all. In fact we intend to help They can also start to save on expensive marketing campaigns as our service is free to use. Companies are only charged a flat rate % on each trip that gets booked, thus they have zero outlay to use JourneyBid.

10Which is better to use, the JourneyBid site or the app?

Both! We are aware our main clients will be in offices and computers, though journeys and bids can be done from both the site and the app.

11How many vehicles does JourneyBid own?

None. We are a broker offering a platform to unite traveling groups with their best transport needs. We do not hold any vehicles of our own nor intend to.

12What is escrow?

An escrow is an arrangement made under contractual provisions between transacting parties, whereby an independent trusted third party receives and disburses money and/or documents for the transacting parties, with the timing of such disbursement by the third party dependent on the fulfillment of contractually-agreed conditions by the transacting parties, or an account established by a broker, under the provisions of license law, for the purpose of holding funds on behalf of the broker's principal or some other person until the consummation or termination of a transaction.
